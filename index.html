About

I'm so happy you've found my website! My internet name is Aroura Borkialis and I am 20 years old. I am currently enrolled in college for IT (Information Technologies) and have a Associates of Liberal Arts Degree as of Fall 2021.

Recently I have begun to open my art to commissions from people on the internet. I have been drawing since I was 6 years old and have grown a strong appriciation for the arts. When I was 12 I was enrolled in a local art class where I began to draw animals. This was the only class I was actually enrolled in till I moved on to High school. From there I quickly grew to love Graphic Design and decided to make it my major. I worked hard and was one of three members to create the first Advanced Graphic Design class in my High School. Finishing up my last years of High School I tookk every single art class avalible and participated in the art club that was offered. Upon graduation I became lost as I was discouraged from becoming a Graphic Designer. To not loose hold of this hobby I offer commissions on my free time for extra practice and a little bit of extra cash.

Choosing an artist to Commission can be a very daunting task but I promise this website will help you navigate the tedious process to be able to have the art piece come out just right. I uphold communicaiton as a very important part of the commission process so you will have a response to any message within two days. Feel free to look around my website and follow my social medias to be updated when quotes open up or when you can have a chance at winning some free art. I look forward to hearing from you soon!

Contact Information

Are you looking for a way to contact me? Well your're in luck as there are multiple way that you can message me. The first way, and best way, would be through any of my social media's. I am currently taking DM's from Facebook, Discord, Telegram, Tiktok, Amino, Snapchat, Twitter and most importantly Instagram. If you do not have social media, which is highly unlikly because that is the only way to find me unless from word of mouth, I have emails. You can email me at thisisanemail@gmail.com or Thisisalsoaemail@yahoo.com.

Before you contact me I request that you do your rersearch and look around this here handy dandy website. It has everything you need to make up the perfect request. First you should start off with the Terms of service. These are my rules which include my refund policies, shipping information, and my boundaries. I will state for you to read them before continuing into any further conversation about my work. Next, decide what you want from the selection. This helps not only me but you as well. Once you've dont those two steps feel free to message me on any of the platforms mentioned above. For all commissions over $20, a government issued ID must be given. This is to make sure the commissioner is over the leagal age of 18 years old to be buying art. If you are not over the age of 18 please have a parent or gaurdian speak on your behalf. This is to avoid any leagal trouble and is non-negotiable.
